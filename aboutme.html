About Me

Who I Am

I am a bioanalytical scientist with a passion for both science and creativity. After completing my doctorate at Utrecht University / NIOZ, where I developed new LC–Orbitrap MS techniques for intramolecular isotope analysis, I continue exploring how advanced analytical chemistry can answer big questions about life, the environment, and technology.

Beyond the lab, I enjoy playing sports, working on creative projects, and collaborating across disciplines. I thrive in international environments and love combining my background in chemistry, microbiology, and data analysis with real-world applications.
